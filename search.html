How to Sync Subtitles

Since we can't detect the exact playback time from embedded players, you need to sync manually:

  1. Load subtitles - Click "Load Subtitles" and choose your language
  2. Start the video - Press play on the embedded player
  3. Click "Start" - Exactly when the movie begins playing
  4. Fine-tune - Use -1s/-0.5s or +0.5s/+1s buttons if subtitles are off
  5. Pause together - When you pause the video, click the subtitle pause button too

Important Limitations

  • Browse/Search Filters: Content filters (hide adult, hide horror) only work on what appears in browse and search results.
  • Video Playback: Once a video plays, it uses third-party embedded players which we cannot control or filter.
  • Subtitle Filtering: Profanity filter only works on our subtitle overlay, not on built-in player subtitles.
  • Subtitle Availability: Not all movies/shows have subtitles in all languages on OpenSubtitles.
